== Version 2.1.0 beta
This beta release uses Bitten's SpellFlash Library version 3.  If you update you will also need to update any of your other Bitten's SpellFlash modules to their latest version, which may also be in beta.
* Balance: Improved travel time detection (for calculating energy gains that are in the air).
* Balance & Resto: Workaround for a bug in the Blizzard UI that sometimes reports the wrong cast time for a spell.
* Bear: Will immediately consider Lacerate as a priority when you cast Pulverize (slight improvement last release, but now it's totally fixed).
* Bear: Checks for the required talents before flashing for Infected Wounds.
* Bear: Bugfix - will no longer flash Berserk for the second between Pulverize going up and Lacerate being consumed.
* Cat: Checks for the required talents before flashing for Blood in the Water
